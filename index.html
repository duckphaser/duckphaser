Duck Phaser Comedy Clubs

Big Night Out comedy at an affordable price in the heart of Swansea's cultural centre. The Garage is the cornerstone of entertainment in the The Uplands. With this performance venue downstairs, the Garage also boasts its game room bar, Whitez upstairs and a swanky late night cocktail party penthouse in The Terrace above that. It's a perfect way to kick your weekend into gear. So come see a show and then stick around for the after party!

Duck Phaser @ The Garage - Thursday 9th April
Freddy Quinne at Duck Phaser Comedy Club.

Freddy Quinne is one of the most sought after acts in the UK. His improvisational skills make him an exciting act to watch and his skills as a compere are so respected he has written a book all about how to master the elusive skill many struggle to develop.

Support comes in the form of Paul Dennis, usually your resident MC but tonight gives you a blast of his skewed philosophies and twisted histories and Dani Johns, tipped by heavyweight Mark Olver as a rising star.

This month's show is glued seamlessly together by the irresistible malcontent, LIAM PICKFORD, whose "scattergun mind" will pepper you with gags you never even realised were possible.
