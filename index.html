Line Up:

David Hoare

An award-winning musical comedian who's been ripping it up all over the place with his punchy songs brimming with silliness and bursting with audacity. With a sell-out Edinburgh run behind him, this might be the last time we can afford him!

'An annoyingly funny young man' (Gary Delaney).

Dani Johns

Quick witted, brutally honest and full of Bristolian charm, Dani Johns is a rising star on the comedy circuit.

With an energetic mix of storytelling and serious over sharing, Dani is an instantly likeable act and described as a “pocket rocket of a comedian” whose “sassy delivery captivates every audience member”.

Off stage, Dani has written for The Guardian and appeared in multiple film and TV projects, including Casualty, Skins and The Sarah Jane Adventures.

"A smart, clever pocket rocket of a comedian who delivers a set full of quick fire gags coupled with a sassy delivery that captivates every audience member." - Crazy Bird Comedy

"Instantly likeable with strong gags to back it up. Dani is one of the South West's most promising rising stars." - Kerfuffle Comedy

"A sweet bouncy stage presence, but don't let the kookiness fool you. Some searing one liners and salacious stories about modern millenial relationships. The audience loved her." - Aftermirth Comedy
